Agh, how to begin the day? <br/>
A list, a to-do list is must,<br/>
A pen & paper to bring first, <br/>
Eight hands, keep them all busy,<br/>
What to do, what not makes me dizzy,<br/>
Attend Madam Jelly fish's pilates,<br/>
All this diet, my belly wobbles, <br/>
Take prescription for the silent but smelly bubbles,<br/>
The oysters having a pearl sale,<br/>
Gift a set to my female,<br/>
Wait, didn't she elope with the muscular whale?<br/>
Cancel buying pearls, oh swell!<br/>
Hunt for crabs, cook myself a quiet dinner,<br/>
Will I ever look leaner?<br/>
Swim for exercise, maybe race a ship,<br/>
Frighten some passengers, that I cannot skip!<br/>
Take Mrs.Dolphin for fin check appointment.<br/>
To hear her surgery and tablet stories, from now I lament.<br/>
Maybe I will just ink my ex,<br/>
To end my day with satisfaction, that's all it takes.<br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
