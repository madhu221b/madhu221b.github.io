That song blasting in my ears, <br/>
Stepping out in rain, no raincoat, so fierce.<br/>
Walked & walked to the unknown, <br/>
Like the lyrics dictated, so lone.<br/>
<br/>
Exhausted not due to the stormy rain,<br/>
But the problems stapled onto me causing pain.<br/>
Shuffled & random like a deck of cards, <br/>
Terrified to face any hurdles, and the blizzards.<br/>
<br/>
"Pick yourself to fight again",<br/>
Maybe all the tears are not in vain.<br/>
Me - my own  umbrella, providing me shelter,<br/>
Standing strong, while life runs helter-skelter.<br/>
<br/>
Like a rock, on the beachside, I stay put,<br/>
The thundering and slapping of waves, now mute.<br/>
I will keep on hoping,<br/>
Not letting the demons get me drooping.<br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
