Saw a man, <br/>
Surveying garbage piles on road,<br/>
Bent on knees,<br/>
Collecting the scrap,<br/>
One man's trash becomes another man's treasure,<br/>
He walking away with pleasure.<br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
