The starving tusked herd marched that day,<br/>
Foraging for  fruits and some hay ,<br/>
Into the village of Yunnan province,<br/>
Oh , to fill their tummies , they found now a means.<br/>

No, they didn’t stumble upon any forest,<br/>
To pluck all the foliage and make it the barest,<br/>
The fourteen elephants found barrels,<br/>
Beaming at their well earned laurels.<br/>

And in those vessels of corn wine, they dipped their trunks,<br/>
These majestic mammals now hopeless drunks,<br/>
Thirty liters of wine they chugged,<br/>
Their faces , now, all cute and mugged.<br/>

The tipsy elephants now wandering lost,<br/>
A tea plantation , they crossed,<br/>
There they passed out,<br/>
Aspirin for a trumpeting hangover ?, now that I doubt.<br/>

— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
