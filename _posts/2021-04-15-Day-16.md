Walked into the store, <br/>
Ice cream! Need I say more ? <br/>
Ducked into the fridge on the floor,<br/>
Shopkeeper waiting to shut its door, <br/>
Hands full with a choco bar and cups four.<br/>
The sun blazing hot,<br/>
Melting my loot, I hope not,<br/>
Need to reach home , in 5 mins dot,<br/>
Raced down the road, with my lot,<br/>
Oh summer, what is this plot ?<br/>
Glad the icy treat stayed fine,<br/>
Binging on it, after dinner, at nine. <br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
