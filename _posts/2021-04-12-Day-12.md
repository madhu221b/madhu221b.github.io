'Boy, these tacos are really spicy!',<br/>
Maybe, I should go easy,<br/>
My stomach rumbled,<br/>
Later unable to sleep, swearing the tacos,<br/>
From the bed I tumbled.<br/>
<br/>
And I did not hit the floor,<br/>
I kept & kept on falling,<br/>
With a thud, landed face down,<br/>
Covered in sand, I found myself.<br/>
'What is happening, is there anyone to help?'<br/>
<br/>
My feet burning, the sun blazing,<br/>
Pyramids & sandstone temples,<br/>
Am I part of the Egyptian fables?<br/>
Hearing someone's feet I hid,<br/>
Tachos - The Pharoah standing in the mid.<br/>
<br/>
A troubled look on his face,<br/>
Which spoke of betrayal.<br/>
Nectanebo II had struck, left his father helpless,<br/>
Tachos now fleeing far away,<br/>
Little did he know, he would be summoned back in chains.<br/>
<br/>
Something brushed against my feet,<br/>
A glowing, black Egyptian cat beneath.<br/>
Rising his golden, yellow eyes, <br/>
Lost in his stare, I fell asleep, <br/>
Back on my bed, I awoke.<br/>
<br/>
In an alternate universe, was I flung?<br/>
Or was it just a dream?<br/>
And then as I got up, <br/>
I was awestruck as I looked below, <br/>
Sand pouring out from my pyjama pockets!<br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
