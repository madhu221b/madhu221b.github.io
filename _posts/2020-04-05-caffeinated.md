Coffee,anodyne of a first date,<br/>
She dashing into the cafe, now late,<br/>
“That french beard has sealed our 2nd date” , she whooped,<br/>
“Dang , I meant apologies for being late!”,said she now stooped.<br/>

Murmurs he , “El que tiene boca se equivoca”,<br/>
Eyeing the table saying , “So smooth tastes the mocha”,<br/>
“Maddy, how do you earn a living ?”<br/>
Calling her by her pet name , so surprising.<br/>

“So Sox, Madhu codes in Java for a daily bread”,<br/>
Her absurd verbal speech turning her red.<br/>

The watch pacing very slowly,<br/>
She wondering, “Recession's going to be holy moly”.<br/>
Bizzare and erratic thoughts ruling her mind,<br/>
To stop it all , someone’s company she seeks to find.<br/>

Now she blurted something ,<br/>
Which also sent her mug cringing,<br/>
“Our kids will be as brown as this latte”,<br/>
“So will you want to be my husband or fiance ?”<br/>

As she utters these words,<br/>
Spilling of coffee on his pants occur,<br/>
The nosy oldie on the next table,<br/>
To contain her curiosity she is now unable.<br/>

Paying the bill she leaves in a hurry,<br/>
The cafe , behind her , now blurry,<br/>
She removes  him from her bag,<br/>
Her “Sox” , her soggy socks of affection,<br/>
Her puppet created through her imagination,<br/>
She was not truly alone,<br/>
For a 2nd date with her puppet she had won<br/>

 
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
