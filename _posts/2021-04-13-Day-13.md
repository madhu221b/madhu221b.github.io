The machine has been finally made,<br/>
'Empathiser' is what it is said, <br/>
One being feels how other being feels <br/>
The butcher felt the pain his blade, <br/>
Delivers as he slices the neck of a chicken.<br/>
The racist felt crushed by the intense hate,<br/>
He spreads for wrong reasons.<br/>
The plastic maker choked,<br/>
Like the marine animals who injest the plastics.<br/>
The abusive husband felt violated,<br/>
Like his wife does night after night.<br/>
The spoilt child who wastes his food,<br/>
Felt the hunger of a malnourished kid.<br/>
The daddy's princess, who only wears 'designer' clothes,<br/>
Felt the struggle of a ragged girl, to keep herself covered.<br/>
The intensity of hatred has reportedly dwindled.<br/>
One of the peace leaders quoted - <br/>
"Shame, we needed a machine, for humans <br/>
  to humanise."<br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
