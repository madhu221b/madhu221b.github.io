'Madhura' meaning sweet. <br/>
Sweet as in the aroma of, <br/>
the tender Madhumalti flowers.<br/>
Sweet as in the ladoos, <br/>
Made by your grandmother.<br/>
Sweet as you exclaim,<br/>
When you touch a baby's cheek.<br/>
Sweet as in the bouquet,<br/>
Your date brings for you.<br/>
Sweet as in your gesture,<br/>
As you help the ajoba into the bus.<br/>
Sweet as in you share your food, <br/>
With the hungry kid bystanding.<br/>
Sweet as in your words,<br/>
As you console a friend,<br/>
Giving shoulder to cry on.<br/>
Sweet as in the apologies,<br/>
For the hurt you might have caused.<br/>
Sweet - is what I try to be.<br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
