My mother labelled everything. <br/>
Her habit to organize, <br/>
stack, pack & label them.<br/>
Catalogued all the fine crockery, <br/>
And the delicate cutlery.<br/>
'The six glasses of falooda',<br/>
'The gifted saaris to exchange with the name of gifters'.<br/>
Every label in detail,<br/>
Nothing ever misplaced,<br/>
Nothing ever lost.<br/>
Adored her organizational skills as I grew up,<br/>
Only to find myself, falling into the same habit. <br/>
Books organized into classics,<br/>
comics, wildlife & mystery.<br/>
Clothes stacked up from,<br/>
least used to most used.<br/>
Boxes sealed with tape,<br/>
the contents labelled.<br/>
We are the projection of those we look up to.<br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
