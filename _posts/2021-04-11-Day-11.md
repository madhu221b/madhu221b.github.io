To myself,<br/>
I heard you were a victim, <br/>
A victim of hypocrisy, <br/>
I heard you wept in the dark, <br/>
And banged your fists on the wall, <br/>
Close friends have taken a  fall? <br/>
I heard you have become cynical, <br/>
Shut the doors & deleted them away, <br/>
In the long run, how does it matter anyway? <br/>
Our mind is like a sponge, <br/>
Absorbs anything you let it soak on.<br/>
Soak it with compassion and goodness, <br/>
Don't walk away from them, <br/>
Walk away from the toxicity with which they came.<br/>
<br/>
Greetings me, <br/>
Your letter reached late, <br/>
It should've been delivered fast,<br/>
Your words made me feel at peace at last.<br/>
When you fall, you get bruised,<br/>
But there are some, who wait in the shadows,<br/>
Like the crutches, they hold & support. <br/>
For some might be the insignificant, little rocks, <br/>
Attempting to block your path. <br/>
But there are more, the angels,<br/>
Who take care of you,<br/>
Shattering these rocks into pieces,<br/>
Do not loose faith in friendship,<br/>
For if one door closes,<br/>
It closes for the good,<br/>
Another many of them open,<br/>
All welcoming and filled with warmth.<br/>
— <i>Madhura Pawar</i>
